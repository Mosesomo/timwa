About Timwa Fisheries

Building Thriving Fish Farms Across Kenya

Timwa Fisheries was born from real farming experience. We've built hundreds of fish ponds across Kenya—from Kisii to Nakuru, Murang'a to Kajiado. Every project taught us what works in our climate, soil, and water conditions.

Today, we partner with individual farmers, cooperatives, and commercial operations to turn land into productive aquaculture systems. Whether it's a half-hectare family pond or a 5-pond cooperative venture, we handle the design, construction, training, and market connections.
